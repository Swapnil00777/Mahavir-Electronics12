About Mahavir Electronics

Our story, our values, our commitment to Muktainagar — आमची कहाणी

Serving Muktainagar Families

Mahavir Electronics and Furniture was founded with one simple goal: to give families in Muktainagar access to quality electronics and furniture at honest prices, without having to travel to a big city. Located conveniently beside Borole Agencies, near JDDC Bank, we have been the go-to destination for home needs in our region.

Over the years, we have grown our collection to include the latest televisions, energy-efficient air conditioners, refrigerators, washing machines, and a wide range of modern furniture — all under one roof. We work directly with authorised distributors to ensure every product is genuine and comes with proper warranty support.

What sets us apart is our personal service. When you walk into our shop, you're not a number — you're a neighbour. We take time to understand your needs and budget and recommend the best option for you. Our team assists with delivery, installation, and after-sales support right here in Muktainagar.
